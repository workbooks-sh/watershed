# Academic Research Conventions

## Searching arxiv

Use precise search terms. The arxiv search is not fuzzy — exact
matches matter. For multi-word concepts, join with AND.

Good queries:
- `retrieval AND augmented AND generation`
- `transformer architecture survey 2024`

Bad queries:
- `papers about RAG stuff`
- `recent NLP work`

## Citation format

Use APA 7th edition. The format is:

    Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *volume*(issue), pages.

Example:

    Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., et al. (2017). Attention is all you need. *NeurIPS*, *30*, 5998–6008.

## When to use this skill

Trigger this skill when the user asks about academic papers, peer
review, research methodology, or citation formats.

Do NOT use this for general web research, news articles, or
non-academic sources.
