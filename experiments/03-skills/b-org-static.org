#+TRIGGERS: papers, citations, peer review, methodology

* Searching arxiv
  Use precise search terms. The arxiv search is not fuzzy — exact
  matches matter. For multi-word concepts, join with AND.

** Good queries
   - =retrieval AND augmented AND generation=
   - =transformer architecture survey 2024=

** Bad queries
   - =papers about RAG stuff=
   - =recent NLP work=

* Citation format
  Use APA 7th edition.

** Format
   Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article.
   /Journal Name/, /volume/(issue), pages.

** Example
   Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., et al. (2017). Attention is
   all you need. /NeurIPS/, /30/, 5998–6008.

* When to use
  Trigger when the user asks about academic papers, peer review,
  research methodology, or citation formats.

  Do NOT use for general web research, news articles, or non-academic
  sources.
